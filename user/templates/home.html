Welcome to BIMS

BIMS is designed to record all the transactions and activities involved in a poultry farm. These solutions integrate Flock Performance, Purchases, Sales, Expenses, Feed Formulation, Feed Production, and Accounting.

Customizable Feed Formulas

Depending on Geographical, Climatic Conditions, Age, and Availability of Raw Materials, different Feed Formulae are adapted by Farmers. Our module helps create cost-effective solutions.
