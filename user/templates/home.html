Welcome to BIMS

BIMS is designed to record all the transactions and activities involved in a poultry farm. These solutions integrate Flock Performance, Purchases, Sales, Expenses, Feed Formulation, Feed Production, and Accounting.

Depending on the Geographical, Climatic Conditions, Age, and Availability of Raw Materials, different Feed Formulae are adapted by the Farmers. A separate module is included in these systems to develop cost-effective formulae.

BIMS keeps track of:

Key Areas
  • Hen house production and standards
  • Vaccination Schedules
  • Cash and Bank Balances
  • Creditors and Debtors
  • Expenditure Details
Additional Tracking Areas
  • Employee Details
  • Shed Details
  • Flock Details
  • Daily Flock entry Details
  • Mortality Ratio
Production and Sales Tracking
  • Production Details
  • Feed Production Unit Details
  • Purchases and Sales Details
  • Medicine and Vaccination Details
  • Stock Information
Financial and Bookkeeping
  • Book Keeping
